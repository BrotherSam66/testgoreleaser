## Ingredients

* 625 g ground hazelnuts
* peel of a lemon
* 2 eggs
* 125 g mix of orangeat and citronat
* 250 g honey
* 250 g sugar

optional:

* 2 g piment
* 3 g cinnamon
* crushed cloves
* baking powder

## Directions

* mix in warm milk
* leave over night
* set on wafers
