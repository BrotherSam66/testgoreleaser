# Cookies

<!-- more -->

## Ingredients

* 200g butter
* 200g sugar
* 400g flour
* 1 egg
* 1 pack vanilla sugar

## Directions

Make a dough from all ingredients.
Let it cool in the fridge, roll out small pieces onto a flat surface and cut some cookies.
Bake golden brown after painting them with egg yolk.
